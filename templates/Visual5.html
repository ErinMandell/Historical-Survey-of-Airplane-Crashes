Fatalities by Operator Type (1918-2018)

Data from the Bureau of Aircraft Accident Archives (BAAA)


More than 10,000 unique operators with crashes are recorded this database, representing a total of 156,000 fatalities.
Of the total fatalities, nearly half (88,000) occured on flights from the top 100 Operators.

Considering the 100 Operators with the most fatalities:
43% of those fatalities have been military related.
